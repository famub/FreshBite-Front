Details

Category: Vegan Salad

Description: A nutritious vegetarian power bowl featuring roasted sweet potatoes, quinoa, chickpeas, avocado, and pomegranate seeds. Topped with a creamy tahini-lemon dressing, this bowl is rich in fiber, plant-based protein, and natural flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed leafy greens (spinach, arugula, or kale)
  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa (white or tricolor)
  • 1 medium sweet potato, peeled, cubed, and roasted
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas (garbanzo beans), rinsed
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
  • 2 tbsp crumbled Feta or Goat cheese (optional)
  • Dressing: 2 tbsp tahini, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp maple syrup

Instructions

  1. Toss the sweet potato cubes in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast at 200¬∞C (400¬∞F) for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  2. Place the washed mixed greens at the bottom of the bowl as a base.
  3. Arrange the cooked quinoa and roasted sweet potatoes on one side.
  4. Place the chickpeas, avocado slices, and pomegranate seeds in sections next to each other.
  5. Sprinkle the pumpkin seeds and crumbled cheese over the top.
  6. Whisk the tahini dressing ingredients together until smooth and drizzle over the bowl.
