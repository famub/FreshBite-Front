Mediterranean Grilled Chicken Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed greens (spinach and romaine)
  • 1 grilled chicken breast
  • The Veggies:
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup English cucumber
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1/3 cup Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup crumbled Feta cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper
  • 2 triangles of warm pita bread on side.

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breast with salt, pepper, and oregano. Grill over medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side until golden brown. Let it rest, then slice.
  2. Wash all vegetables. Slice the cucumbers into rounds, halve the tomatoes, and slice the onions into thin rings.
  3. Place the mixed greens at the bottom of the bowl. Arrange the veggies and chicken in distinct sections on top.
  4. Sprinkle the feta cheese and olives over the center.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice just before eating. Serve with warm pita bread on the side.
