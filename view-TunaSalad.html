Mediterranean Tuna Salad

Sara Ahmed

Details

Category: Seafood Salad

Description: A light and refreshing Mediterranean-style tuna salad that combines fresh flavors with a simple, healthy preparation. Perfect for a quick meal, this salad is easy to make, satisfying, and ideal for anyone looking for a nutritious and delicious option.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1 medium cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 tbsp sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tbsp olives, sliced
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp salt( or to taste)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Drain the tuna well and place it in a large bowl.
  2. Add the chickpeas, cucumber, pepper, red onion, sun-dried tomatoes, olives, and parsley.
  3. Season with garlic powder, oregano, and salt.
  4. Drizzle olive oil over the salad.
  5. Mix everything thoroughly until all ingredients are evenly combined.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
